Customer 360 :Customer segmentation according to 3 main aspects RFM (short for Recency, Frequency, Monetary) in which:

  • Recency (R): the last time the customer used the service
  • Frequency (F): frequency of service use by customers
  • Monetary (M): revenue from customers

RFM model analyzes based on these 3 indicators, scores are scored from 1 to 4 in which 1 is the lowest and 4 is the highest.
